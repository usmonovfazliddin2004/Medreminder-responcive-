Medreminder

Medreminder is a free pill reminder and medication tracker app. You may make doctor's appointments, learn more about the medications you're taking and their side effects, track your health, and share your health status with your family and friends. Up to three people profiles can be added in an account at once, and anyone from anywhere in the world can view your loved one's account by invitation. Join the hundreds of satisfied users who see our app as the greatest of all medication reminder apps.
